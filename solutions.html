ETW Solution

Transforming Your Strategy Into an Agile Execution Plan with ETW

ETW's cloud-based application brings together all of the necessary functionality to drive the execution of your strategy. Assign individual goals that align to the success of a major objective, track and display key performance indicators at all levels of your execution plan, quickly see the status of all objectives throughout your entire organization and drill-down to the results, strategic thinking, and coaching of individual employees.

Build Your Plan


Begin populating the top-level execution plan dashboard with the strategy elements you are targeting, key performance indicators that will let you know whether the organization is winning or losing, and the operative groups that represent how you collect the major objectives that need to be accomplished.



Align Individual Goals To Your Strategy


Create major objectives within each operative group that associate with one or more of your strategy elements. Assign owners, define success criteria, set due dates and required udpate intervals so expectations are 100% clear.





Track What Matters

Keep your finger on the pulse of the business with real-time dashboards. Drill into status and trends to quickly identify gaps and course correct if needed.



Drive An Intentional Culture


Use ETW's culture module to define and communciate the tools that align your organization. Keep the mission, vision and behaviors that make your organization great top-of-mind by displaying them on employee dashboards as an interactive discussion card.
